Inktober 2017

Every October, artists all over the world take on the Inktober drawing challenge by doing one ink drawing a day the entire month.


I decided to take up the challenge in a slightly different way. Instead of ink, I used Processing to generate the art through code. I have tried to create visuals that would otherwise have been difficult to be done manually.
